    Home Soon

    Between May-August 2021, The Tamir Rice Foundation and Khoury (via SPACES) partnered to facilitate a city-wide art supply drive. Four collection bins, adorned by local artists with paintings that celebrate Tamir's life, were installed throughout Greater Cleveland. Donated supplies went toward workshops facilitated by teaching artists and members of the Cuyahoga County Jail Coalition, Josiah and James Quarles. The art featured in this exhibit was produced by youth ages 10-21 detained in the Cuyahoga County Detention Center alongside students from Mayfair Elementary School. All remaining supplies went toward the Detention Center, a holiday art box giveaway, and future Tamir Rice Foundation art programs.

    SPACES, Cleveland, Ohio. 2022.
